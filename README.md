# Bank Marketing Campaign Analysis (Excel Dashboard)

## Overview

This project analyzes a bank marketing dataset to identify key factors influencing customer subscription to term deposits using Microsoft Excel.

---

## Business Problem
The bank aims to improve its marketing campaign effectiveness by identifying customer segments most likely to subscribe to term deposits.

---

## Objective

To evaluate campaign performance and uncover insights related to customer behavior, targeting strategy, and conversion trends.

---

## Data Preparation

* Cleaned and transformed raw dataset (45K+ records)
* Handled missing/unknown values
* Created new features:

  * Age Groups
  * Balance Buckets
  * Subscription (Numeric)

---

## Analysis Performed

* Customer segmentation by **Age Group** and **Job Role**
* Channel performance analysis (**Contact Method**)
* Seasonal trend analysis (**Monthly Conversion**)
* Financial behavior analysis (**Account Balance Buckets**)

---

## Key Metrics
- Total Customers: 45,212
- Total Conversions: 5,289
- Conversion Rate: 11.7%
- Avg Account Balance: ₹1,362
- 
---

## Key Insights

* Students (~28.6%) and retired customers (~22.7%) showed highest conversion rates
* Cellular contact method performed better than telephone
* March (~51%) recorded the highest conversion rate
* Higher balance customers had better subscription likelihood

---

## Dashboard Features

* Interactive Excel dashboard
* KPI Cards:

  * Total Customers
  * Total Conversions
  * Conversion Rate
  * Avg Account Balance
* Visualizations:

  * Conversion by Age Group
  * Conversion by Job Role
  * Conversion by Contact Method
  * Monthly Conversion Trend
* Slicers for dynamic filtering:

  * Job Role
  * Education Level
